## From Idea to Fabric: The Fabric Design Workflow

The fabric design process typically involves the following steps:

1.  **Inspiration & Research:** Gathering inspiration from various sources, such as nature, art, and fashion trends.
2.  **Concept Development:** Brainstorming ideas and sketching out initial designs.
3.  **Digital Design:** Creating the final design using digital design software.
4.  **Color Selection:** Choosing the appropriate color palette for the design.
5.  **Pattern Repeat:** Creating a seamless repeating pattern.
6.  **File Preparation:** Preparing the design for printing, including specifying resolution, color profiles, and file format.
7.  **Printing:** Printing the design onto fabric using the appropriate printing method.
8.  **Evaluation:** Evaluating the printed fabric for color accuracy, print quality, and overall appearance.
